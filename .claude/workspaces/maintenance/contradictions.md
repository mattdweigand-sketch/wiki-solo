---

## How to use this page

- **Add an entry** when ingesting a source that disagrees with an existing wiki page, or when lint surfaces a conflict.
- **Update an entry** when a new source resolves it, weakens it, or strengthens one side.
- **Close an entry** only when the disagreement is resolved with `confidence: high` (a definitive source). Otherwise, keep it open and adjust the lean.
- Each entry names the **two (or more) positions**, the **sources** behind each, the **affected wiki pages**, and the **current lean** (which side, if any, the wiki currently favors and why).

Format every entry as below. Sort newest at the top.
